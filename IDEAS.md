## 💰 Idea #1: Organization Bounty System & Solution Data Marketplace

**Date**: 2026-03-31  
**Status**: Concept

### The Concept

Organizations (companies, NGOs, governments) post their **unsolvable/hard problems** on SolvingHub with a **bounty** attached. The platform then creates value in **two ways**:

### Revenue Stream 1 — Problem Hunting (Bounty Board)

- Organizations post problems on a dedicated **"Problem Hunting"** page
- Any user can attempt to solve the problem
- If a solution is accepted by the organization → **solver gets paid**
- Think: HackerOne for real-world problems, not just bugs

### Revenue Stream 2 — AI-Powered Solution Intelligence & Data Sales

- Every proposed solution (even rejected ones) generates **valuable data**
- Platform AI aggregates and analyzes *all* proposed solutions to find patterns, synthesize a broader perspective, and generate a **meta-solution** that no single person could create alone
- This aggregated intelligence is **sold back to organizations** as a premium report
- Organizations get: crowd-sourced thinking + AI synthesis = a "broader mind" on their problem

### Why This Is Powerful

| Aspect | Value |
|--------|-------|
| **For Solvers** | Real money for solving real problems — strong incentive to join |
| **For Organizations** | Access to global brain-power + AI synthesis they can't get internally |
| **For SolvingHub** | Commission on bounties + data/report sales = **dual revenue model** |
| **Data Moat** | The more people propose solutions, the better the AI gets → **network effect** |

### Honest Assessment

**Strengths:**
- ✅ Solves the monetization question (bounties + data sales)
- ✅ Gives users a *financial* reason to come to the platform (not just social)
- ✅ The AI aggregation angle is genuinely novel — no platform does "crowd wisdom + AI synthesis" well
- ✅ Creates a flywheel: more bounties → more solvers → better data → more orgs → more bounties

**Risks to Think About:**
- ⚠️ **Data privacy/ethics**: Users must explicitly consent to their solutions being used in aggregated reports. Transparency is key.
- ⚠️ **Quality control**: Need a system to prevent low-effort/spam solutions just to game the bounty
- ⚠️ **Legal**: Bounty contracts, IP ownership of solutions — who owns the solution? The solver or the platform?
- ⚠️ **Cold start**: Need enough solvers before orgs will pay. Consider **free tier** for orgs initially to build the solver community.

### Potential Implementation Approach

```
Organization posts problem + bounty ($$$)
        ↓
Appears on "Problem Hunting" page (separate from community problems)
        ↓
Solvers propose solutions (time-limited or open-ended)
        ↓
    ┌───────────────────┐
    │  Two things happen │
    └───────────────────┘
        ↓                    ↓
   Org reviews &         AI aggregates ALL
   accepts a winner      proposed solutions
   → Solver gets paid    → Generates intelligence report
   → Platform takes %    → Sold to org (premium)
```
